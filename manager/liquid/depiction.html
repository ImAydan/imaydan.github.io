liquid - New but familiar

Look up, it's looking at you. It was always there and it hasn't really changed since iOS 7. It's your statusbar!
"liquid" doesn't only theme that, but also the CC, haptic touch menues and the quick action buttons on the lockscreen. Tweaks like CCModules are fully supportet (even when interacting with them).

Usage

The theme can be applied with both iThemer and SnowBoard. SnowBoard requires multiple extension, like the UI extension and the status bar extension, from SparkDev's Repo.
Icon requests are accepted, just contact me with one of the buttons at the end of the depiction.
If you are interested in making a UI theme I will create a UI theming tutorial soon.
