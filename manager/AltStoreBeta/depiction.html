AltStore Beta

Do you still remember the good old days? When themes stretched over your whole device and were extremely high quality?
Well, I hope this is a step in the right direction.
My all-new theme "cake" is modern, intuitive, themes 200 icons and stuff like the dock, badges and page dots. The icon mask and overlay distinct your icons even more.

Usage

SparkDev's Repo.
As long as this theme is avaible on this repo it is still in beta and will be completely free. Icon requests are accepted, just contact me with one of the buttons at the end of the depiction.
When I think that "cake" is ready I will release it on either Twickd or Dynastic for a small price.
